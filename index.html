These applications allow you to share data seamlessly across multiple devices! Write a document in Google Docs at school and open it on a different computer at home!

Social networks are another example of a cloud application!

When we share our photos or post our information, we are uploading our data to the cloud!

Our data is then stored on servers across the planet, which we can then access through the internet at any time!
